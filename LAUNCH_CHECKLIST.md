# Local Lead Generation Launch Checklist

Use this checklist for each new local lead-generation website.

## 1. Choose The Niche And City

Pick one service and one city.

Good examples:

- `nettoyage-canape-cergy.fr`
- `plombier-pontoise.fr`
- `serrurier-osny.fr`

The niche should have:

- local search demand
- commercial intent
- phone-driven customer behavior
- local providers who may buy leads

## 2. Buy The Domain

Prefer a clear service + city domain when testing demand.

Example:

`nettoyage-canape-cergy.fr`

## 3. Build A Static Website

Keep the site simple:

- fast loading
- mobile-first
- static pages
- no unnecessary app logic
- no CMS for the first version

Required pages:

- homepage
- thank-you page
- legal notice
- privacy policy
- sitemap
- robots.txt

## 4. Add Lead Capture

Use:

- phone CTA
- SMS option
- quote/contact form

Use one dedicated phone number consistently across:

- website
- Google Business Profile
- directories
- outreach material

## 5. Write Honest Copy

The website should clearly collect service requests.

Avoid:

- fake reviews
- fake address
- fake company story
- guaranteed intervention claims

Use wording such as:

- "Demandez une estimation gratuite."
- "Aucun paiement n'est demandé sur ce site."
- "Une solution peut être proposée selon disponibilité."

## 6. SEO Basics

Homepage should include:

- service + city H1
- clear meta title
- clear meta description
- FAQ section
- local area mentions
- semantic HTML
- optimized image

Create:

- `sitemap.xml`
- `robots.txt`

Submit the sitemap in Google Search Console.

## 7. Google Search Console

Set up:

- domain property
- sitemap submission
- indexing request

Monitor:

- impressions
- clicks
- search queries
- average position

## 8. Google Business Profile

Use a real phone number and consistent business details.

Recommended setup:

- business type: service-area business
- category: cleaning service or niche-specific equivalent
- service areas: start local
- website URL: the local domain
- phone number: same as website

Do not use fake addresses, fake reviews, or misleading business information.

## 9. Tracking

Track:

- phone clicks
- SMS clicks
- form submissions
- thank-you page views
- Search Console impressions
- Search Console clicks

The goal is to understand whether the site attracts real demand.

## 10. First 90 Days

Do not over-optimize too early.

Focus on:

- indexing
- impressions
- clicks
- calls
- SMS messages
- form submissions
- lead quality

Do not:

- redesign constantly
- create many thin AI pages
- buy spam backlinks
- change domain
- pivot before search data is available

## 11. After Validation

If the site receives impressions, clicks, and leads:

- contact local providers
- offer pay-per-lead
- offer monthly rental
- repeat the model in another city or niche

If the site gets impressions but no leads:

- improve CTA placement
- improve form clarity
- test phone-first layout
- refine the offer

If the site gets no impressions:

- check indexing
- improve content
- add local relevance
- wait for more data before abandoning the niche
