Local Lead Generation Website Launch Checklist

Reusable Process For Every New Website

Use this checklist every time you launch a new local lead-generation website.

Examples:

* nettoyage-canape-cergy.fr
* plombier-pontoise.fr
* serrurier-osny.fr
* climatisation-argenteuil.fr

⸻

1. DOMAIN & HOSTING

Buy Domain

Choose:

* exact match local domain
    OR
* local brand domain

Examples:

* nettoyage-canape-cergy.fr
* plombier-pontoise.fr

⸻

Setup Hosting

Recommended:

* Vercel
* Cloudflare
* Netlify

Requirements:

* HTTPS
* fast loading
* mobile optimized

⸻

2. WEBSITE SETUP

Required Pages

Homepage

Must include:

* service + city
* phone CTA
* WhatsApp CTA
* form
* reviews
* FAQ
* local content
* before/after photos

⸻

Optional Pages

* contact
* FAQ
* city pages
* service pages

3. SEO BASICS

Meta Title

Example:
Nettoyage Canapé Cergy - Nettoyage à domicile

⸻

Meta Description

Example:
Nettoyage professionnel de canapés à domicile à Cergy et alentours. Devis gratuit et intervention rapide.

⸻

Heading Structure

* H1 = main keyword
* H2 = services
* H2 = FAQ
* H2 = zones desservies

⸻

Internal SEO

Must include:

* city names
* service keywords
* semantic HTML
* optimized images
* mobile-first design

⸻

4. SITEMAP

Generate sitemap.xml

Required:

* homepage
* all public pages

/sitemap.xml

⸻
