# Project Context

## Site

`nettoyage-canape-cergy.fr`

## Purpose

This is the first MVP site for the local lead-generation model.

The goal is to validate whether a focused local SEO website can attract search traffic for a specific service and city, then convert that traffic into calls, SMS messages, and form submissions.

## Positioning

This site is a local lead-capture asset, not a fake artisan company.

Use honest wording:

- request an estimate
- no payment on the site
- response subject to availability
- no guaranteed intervention

## Rules

- Static site only.
- Mobile-first.
- Keep phone and form CTAs prominent.
- Do not add fake reviews.
- Do not add a fake address.
- Do not make fake company claims.
- Do not create thin programmatic pages.
- Do not overbuild before Search Console data exists.
