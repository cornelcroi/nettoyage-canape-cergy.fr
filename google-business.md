# Google Business Profile Notes

Use these notes when creating or maintaining a Google Business Profile for a local lead-generation MVP.

## Purpose

The Google Business Profile should support the same validation goal as the website:

- prove local demand
- make phone/SMS contact easy
- keep business details consistent
- help users understand the service area

## Business Type

Use a service-area business setup.

Do not list a fake public address.

## Suggested Category

For `nettoyage-canape-cergy.fr`, use the closest accurate category available in Google Business Profile.

Possible categories:

- Services de nettoyage
- Service de nettoyage de tissus d'ameublement
- Service de nettoyage de tapis

Choose the most accurate category that Google offers in the dashboard.

## Service Areas

Start local.

Examples:

- Cergy
- Pontoise
- Osny
- Éragny
- Vauréal
- Saint-Ouen-l'Aumône

Do not add a huge region at the beginning. The goal is local relevance.

## Phone Number

Use the same number as the website:

`+33 7 56 80 43 67`

Use this number consistently across:

- website
- Google Business Profile
- directories
- citations
- provider outreach

Nettoyage de canapés, fauteuils, matelas et textiles à domicile à Cergy et dans les communes proches. Demande d'estimation gratuite, contact par téléphone, SMS ou formulaire, selon disponibilité des prestataires locaux.

## Voicemail Script

Bonjour et merci pour votre appel.

Veuillez laisser votre nom, votre ville et une courte description de votre demande de nettoyage textile. Vous pouvez également envoyer des photos par SMS.

Nous reviendrons vers vous rapidement.

## Photos

Use only realistic and relevant photos.

Avoid:

- fake team photos
- fake premises
- generic stock photos that do not match the service

Acceptable photo types:

- clean living-room textile context
- cleaning equipment
- real service photos once available
- simple brand or website visuals

## Important Rules

Do not use:

- fake reviews
- fake address
- fake opening claims
- misleading company history
- exaggerated guarantees

Keep the profile consistent with the website: a local service request and lead-capture asset designed to validate demand.
